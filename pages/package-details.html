Bali Honeymoon Escape

📍 Bali, Indonesia ★★★★★ 4.9 (1,247 reviews) 🌙 6 Nights / 7 Days

Tour Overview

Escape to the Island of the Gods on this 6-night romantic getaway crafted for couples. Wake up to lush rice terraces in Ubud, sail to Nusa Penida's cliffs, and end your trip in a beachfront villa in Seminyak. Every detail — from candle-lit dinners to private transfers — is taken care of.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 · Arrival in Denpasar → Ubud

Welcome at DPS airport. Private transfer to your villa in Ubud. Evening welcome dinner at a rice-paddy restaurant.

Day 2 · Ubud Cultural Tour

Tegalalang rice terraces, sacred Monkey Forest, traditional Balinese spa session (90 min). Optional Kecak fire dance in the evening.

Day 3 · Mount Batur Sunrise & Hot Springs

Pre-dawn trek to Mount Batur summit. Sunrise breakfast. Relax at natural hot springs. Return for leisure afternoon.

Day 4 · Transfer to Seminyak

Drive south to Seminyak. Check-in to beachfront villa. Sunset cocktails at Potato Head Beach Club.

Day 5 · Nusa Penida Day Trip

Speedboat to Nusa Penida. Visit Kelingking Beach, Angel's Billabong & Broken Beach. Snorkel with manta rays.

Day 6 · Seminyak Free Day + Candlelight Dinner

Free day for shopping at Seminyak boutiques. Romantic candlelight dinner on the beach.

Day 7 · Departure

Private transfer to DPS airport. Farewell with gift hamper.
